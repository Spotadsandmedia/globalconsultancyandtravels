BANK LOAN

A bank loan is a borrowed sum of money with interest that must be repaid over a set period of time. It can be secured or unsecured and is offered by banks for personal, business, or other purposes.

UNSECURED LOAN

An unsecured loan is a type of loan that does not require collateral. It is based on the borrower's creditworthiness and ability to repay.

PRIVATE LOAN

A private loan is a loan provided by an individual or a non-bank entity, rather than a traditional financial institution. The terms and conditions of private loans can vary widely based on the lender's preferences and the borrower's credit worthiness.
